## 🏢 Business Problem

> High revenue growth alone does **not** guarantee business quality.

For an e-commerce platform like Nykaa, sustainable growth depends on five pillars:

> **1.** Customer Acquisition Efficiency
> **2.** Order Frequency Growth
> **3.** Revenue Monetization per User
> **4.** Segment Economics — Beauty vs Fashion
> **5.** Profitability Expansion at Scale

**Objective:** Determine whether Nykaa's growth is sustainable and identify the true business drivers behind its performance.

### 📍 Page 5 — Strategic Recommendations

> **Business Question:** What should Nykaa focus on to sustain future growth?

![Page 5 - Strategic Recommendations](Dashboard%20Screenshots/Page5_Strategic_Recommendations.png)

**1️⃣ Strengthen Beauty Leadership**
Expand premium product offerings · Deepen brand partnerships · Improve customer loyalty & retention programs

**2️⃣ Improve Fashion Monetization**
Increase Average Order Value (AOV) · Expand premium fashion categories · Improve cross-sell opportunities with Beauty

**3️⃣ Increase Customer Lifetime Value**
Improve repeat purchase frequency · Enhance personalization & recommendation engines · Strengthen loyalty programme mechanics

**4️⃣ Maintain Profitable Growth**
Continue margin expansion · Focus on operational efficiency · Balance topline growth with bottom-line discipline

> 💡 **Executive Takeaway:** Future growth should come from stronger monetization, improved retention, and disciplined profitability expansion.
